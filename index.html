Guess Who?

How to Play:

  1. Each player must open the game in one device;
  2. Click on the logo of the game to see your card (don't show to the other players);
  3. Click again to hide your card;
  4. Make Yes/No questions to guess your oponent's card;
  5. If you want to hide a character on your board, just click on the character;
  6. The winner is the one who guesses right your oponent's card first

Be carefull: Your oponent can have the same card as you!
